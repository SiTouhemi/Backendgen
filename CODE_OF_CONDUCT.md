# Code of Conduct

We commit to a harassment-free, welcoming project for everyone. Be respectful, assume good intent, give actionable technical feedback, and avoid personal attacks, discrimination, sexualized conduct, threats, or publishing private information.

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and temporarily or permanently exclude participants whose behavior harms the community. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Maintainers will protect reporter privacy as far as reasonably possible and apply responses proportionately.

This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project publicly. It is based on the principles of the Contributor Covenant 2.1 without copying its enforcement contact placeholders.
